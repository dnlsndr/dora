Here are some of the critics' comments about dora:

"Dora's love for this art form, the bhakti with which she has worshiped it was revealed in every moment, gesture, stance and expression." (TIMES OF INDIA, Ahmedabad)

"A gifted dancer who has overcome the alien german cultural backdrop admirably to chime in with the spirit of an indian dance idiom. Dora has dance, line and technique well under control." (Leela Venkataraman, THE HINDU, Delhi)

"Like a beautiful Kashmere belle she moved trough aamad, thaat, tukra et al, with skilful grasp of changing laya and bodyline." (Shanta Serbjeet Singh, HINDUSTAN TIMES, Delhi)

"The unspectular expressiveness of her flowing movements - she literally has every musclefibre under control - renders the presentation of her classical Indian Kathakdance to an artistic peak performance." (GÜNZBURGER ZEITUNG)

"In harmonious body language and with shining eyes, reflecting every sensation undisguised longing as well as hesitation, Dora opened her evening 'KATHAK', in which grace, energy and rhythm were found combined in perfect-beautiful expression." (SÜDDEUTSCHE ZEITUNG)
